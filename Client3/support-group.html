Why would you join the support groups?

A support group provides a secure environment where you may acquire practical, constructive, and useful knowledge. You'll get some encouragement, and you'll learn more about how to deal with your challenges from other people's experiences. Hearing from others who are going through similar problems will help you feel less alone.

When it's not convenient to meet in person, online support groups might be helpful. If the distance is too terrific, transportation is unavailable, or your job or family schedule prohibits you from joining an in-person support group, they're a pretty great option. And above all of that while having an injury its difficult for you to go out or move from your place, so having this meeting online will be your best option.
